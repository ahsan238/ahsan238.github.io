About

I am a second year PhD student at NC State and I am currently being supervised by Dr. Anupam Das at the Wolfpack Security and Privacy (WSPR) Lab. Specifically, I work to enhance web measurement tools that inform us the web interactions in terms of digital privacy and online tracking more transparently.
